Q1. What is Spencers Rewards?

Spencers Rewards is a paid subscription-based reward program. Members earn cashback based on their monthly spend at Spencers.

  • Pay just ₹500/year
  • Earn up to ₹600/month cashback
  • Save up to ₹7,200 annually!
Q2. How much does it cost?

The annual subscription fee is just ₹500.

Q3. How does it work?

Earn rewards Cashback basis as per below table

Monthly Spend (₹) Monthly Cashback Limit Rate Annual Cashback Limit
₹3,300 - ₹4,000 ₹100 3% ₹1,200
₹4,001 - ₹5,000 ₹200 4% ₹2,400
₹5,001 - ₹10,000 & above ₹600 6% ₹7,200
Q4. Where can I use it?

Your membership works at:

  • Spencers stores
  • Jiffy (Spencers Quick Commerce App)
  • And ordered on Phone

Q5. How can I enroll?

You can enroll at the billing counter. Pay ₹500 at checkout and complete a quick digital registration on https://rewards.spencersretail.com/

Q6. What information is required for registration?
  • Name
  • Mobile Number (mandatory)
  • Email (optional)
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
Q7. How will I know if my membership is active?

You’ll receive:

  • WhatsApp/SMS confirmation
  • OTP-based login for wallet access

Q8. How do I earn rewards cashback?

Spend monthly and hit the eligible thresholds to earn rewards cashback.
Example: Spend ₹5,000 → Get 4% cashback (Max Rs. 200 monthly)

Q9. Can I earn rewards cashback on all items?

Most items, except: Liquor, Tobacco, Baby Food, Gift Cards & carry bags.

Q10. Is there any quantity limit on any Item for cashback issuance?

Yes, there are certain quantity limits as below:

  • Flour: For pack sizes of 1kg & below - 10 Units per month & Pack sizes above 1kg - 3 Units per month.
  • Oil/Ghee: For pack sizes of 1L & below - 10 Units per month & Pack sizes above 1L - 3 Units per month.
  • Sugar: For pack sizes of 1kg & below - 10 Units per month & Pack sizes above 1kg - 3 Units per month.
Q11. When is rewards cashback credited?

On the 1st Day of the next month.
Example: June spend = Cashback on July 1

Q12. How do I track my rewards cashback?
  • OTP login to rewards page to check cashback, progress & passbook.
  • Via WhatsApp/SMS updates
  • Assistance from cashier at the time of billing.

Q13. How do I redeem rewards cashback in-store?
  • Give your registered mobile number at billing
  • System checks wallet balance
  • You receive an OTP
  • Enter OTP → Cashback applied instantly
Q14. Can I redeem rewards cashback on all items?

Most items, except: Tobacco, Baby Food, Gift Cards & carry bags.

Q15. Can I use cashback partially, or must I redeem it all at once?

Yes, you can use any available cashback amount full or partial if your cart value covers it.

Q16. What happens if I return items after earning cashback?

In case of returns, the corresponding cashback earned may be adjusted or reversed.

Q17. How long is my membership valid?

Your membership is valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.

Q18. What happens if I don’t use my cashback before the expiry?

You can use your unused wallet balance within the next 30 days post membership expiration.

Q19. How do I renew my subscription?
  • Renew at billing by paying ₹500 for the next year
  • Or get free renewal if your eligible spend exceeds ₹60,000 annually

Q20. Can I gift the membership?

Not at the moment. Membership is tied to your mobile number.

Q21. Can I get a refund for my membership?

No. As per policy, the ₹500 membership fee is non-refundable.

Q22. Can I cancel or transfer it?

No. Membership is non-cancellable and non-transferable.

Q23. Why is my wallet amount different across app and store?

There may be a short delay in sync. Wallet balances are reconciled every 24 hours. You’ll also see the “Last Updated” timestamp.

Q24. What happens if I change my mobile number?

Currently, the membership is linked to your registered number. Please contact customer support for assistance.
