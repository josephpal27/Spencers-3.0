Q1. What is Spencers Rewards?

Spencers Rewards is a paid subscription based loyalty. Members earn cashback based on their monthly spend at Spencers.

  • Pay just ₹500/year
  • Earn up to ₹600/month cashback
  • Save up to ₹7,200 annually!
Q2. How much does it cost?

The annual subscription fee is just ₹500.

Q4. Where can I use it?

Your membership works at:

  • Spencers physical stores
  • Jiffy app (online orders)
  • Phone Delivery

Q5. How can I enroll?

You can enroll at the billing counter. Pay ₹500 at checkout and complete a quick digital registration on https://rewards.spencersretail.com/

Q6. What information is required for registration?
  • Name
  • Mobile Number (mandatory)
  • Email (optional)
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
Q7. How will I know my membership is active?

You’ll receive:

  • WhatsApp/SMS confirmation
  • OTP-based login for wallet access

Q8. How do I earn cashback?

Spend monthly and hit the eligible thresholds to earn cashback..
Example: Spend ₹5,000 → Get 4% cashback.

Q9. Can I earn cashback on all items?

Most items, except: Liquor, Tobacco, Baby Food, Gift Cards & select Consumables

Q10. What are quantity limits?
  • Flour: 10 Units (≤1kg) or 3 Units (>1kg)
  • Oil/Ghee: 10 Units (≤1L) or 3 Units (>1L)
  • Sugar: 10 Units (≤1kg) or 3 Units (>1kg)

On the 1st Day of the next month.

  • OTP login to rewards page to check cashback, progress & passbook.

  • Assistance from cashier at the time of billing.

Q15. How long is my membership valid?

Your membership is valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
